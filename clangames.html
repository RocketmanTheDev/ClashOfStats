Clan Games explained

Clan games take place once a month, starting on 22th and ending on the 28th. They last 6 days and every member of the clan has a variety of challenges to choose from. The completon of each challenge rewards the player with a certain ammount of points, depending on the difficulty of the challenge. Every player has a maximum points they can acquire for the clan. Depending on the ammount of points collectively scored, everyone is rewarded with a range of resourses and magic items. The clan needs at least 15 players to max their points for all rewards to unlock and clan members who score at least 80% get an extra reward.
