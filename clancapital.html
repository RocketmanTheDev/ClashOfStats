Clan Capital explained

Every friday marks the start of the weekly raid weekend. In order to participate you need to be in a clan that is at least level 2 and the weekend must be started by the leader or one of the co-leaders. Each participant has 5 attacks and a variety of unique troops to attack the clan capital bases of enemies. If you manage to 3 star a village within your five attacks you get a one time bonus attack. The objective is to collect as much clan capital gold as possible, which is used to upgrade the army, spells, defence and hall buildings.
