Clan War Leagues explained

When the Clan War Leagues begin, there is a two-day sign-up period. During this period, either a leader or a co-leader can sign up his Clan and must pick at least 15 players to battle. Anyone in the clan can participate in this war and the roster of players can be changed for every battle. The war league is one week long, during which, different clans batttle every day and the winner is the clan, that scores the most stars during this week (Destruction percent is used as tie breaker)
