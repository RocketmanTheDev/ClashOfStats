Clan War Leagues explained

When the Clan War Leagues begin, there will be a two-day sign-up period. During this period, the leader or co-leader who enters the Clan into signing up must choose a roster of players who will enter the League. The roster must consist of at least 15 players, though the roster can include everyone in the Clan , provided they are eligible for war.
