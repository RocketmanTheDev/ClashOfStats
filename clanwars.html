Clan Wars explained

Clan Wars vary by the size of the battling clans, starting from 5 vs 5 all the way up to 50 vs 50. The members that go to war are determined by the leader or co-leader who starts the war search. The objective is to collet as much stars as possible by attacking the enemy war bases. The maximum stars per attack are 3 and each member can attack twice in a single war. In the end, the clan with the most stars is victorious.
